Three commitments

What you will do

Three specific, time-bound actions you commit to this week. Not "study more" or "do better." Concrete behaviors you will either do or not do, with a way to know whether they happened.

What makes a good commitment

Specific behavior, not outcome. Time-bound. Achievable this week. Example: "Spend 30 minutes every Tuesday and Thursday after class doing retrieval practice on this week's concepts." Not "study harder." Not "understand endocrine."
