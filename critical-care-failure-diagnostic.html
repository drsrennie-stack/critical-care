Why you're failing, more specifically.

Most students who fail A&P don't fail for one reason. They fail for a specific combination of reasons, and the recovery path depends on which reasons dominate. This diagnostic looks at four dimensions of academic failure and produces a profile specific to you.

What this diagnostic does

24 questions across 4 dimensions: how you spend time, how you study, what content you understand, and what's happening emotionally. Takes about 8 to 10 minutes. At the end, you'll get a profile showing your primary and secondary failure patterns, specific sub-patterns within them, and the first actions most likely to matter.

How to answer

Be truthful, not aspirational. Rate how true each statement is right now, in your current A&P experience, not how you wish it was or how you plan to change. The profile is only useful if the input is honest.
