Grade Audit

Where you actually stand, in numbers. A&P courses are usually graded by category (lecture exams, lab exams, quizzes, and so on). Enter your categories the way your syllabus describes them, then add the assessments you've taken and the ones still to come.

Before you start

Open your syllabus and your gradebook. The syllabus tells you the category weights. The gradebook tells you your scores. Both are needed. Accuracy here matters because the output is only as honest as the input.

How to use this

In any category above, enter a score for a remaining assessment. It's automatically treated as a prediction rather than a real score. Be realistic, not aspirational. Unpredicted assessments are assumed to match the average of your predictions.
